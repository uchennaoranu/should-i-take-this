Decision Filter

Should I Take This?

A fast structural filter before you commit.

1. Does this create any form of lock-in?

Examples: exclusivity, long contract, switching cost

2. Can you exit cleanly without penalty?

3. Does this make you dependent on a single party, income, or outcome?

4. Is the upside capped while downside remains open?

5. Are you committing more than you are guaranteed to receive?

6. If this fails, can you return to your current position easily?

Please answer all 6 questions.

Result

    This is a structural decision filter, not advice.
